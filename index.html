Why buy Bitcoin?

Bitcoin is becoming one of the most sought-after assets in the world. Being created in the wake of the 2008 financial crisis to combat governments' unlimited money printing and debasement of their currency, bitcoin strives to be the most pristine asset ever created. Bitcoin achieves ultimate scarcity due to the fact that there will be 21 million coins ever created (it's even scarcer than gold). It is also on pace to grow faster than the internet in 1998 (current projection of users is at 130 million). One of the biggest reasons why to buy bitcoin is that it's completely decentralized. No single person, group, or government can control it or tell you what to do with it. In times of uncertainty( like the invasion of Ukraine), bitcoin is always there and never shut down, or frozen by the banks because that country's currency is collapsing.
